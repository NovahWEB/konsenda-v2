The field index

Naturalist plates, species by species

San Blas holds over 400 species; Bocas del Toro, a hundred morphs of a single frog. Here are some you can find, treated as plates from a field notebook. Tap each plate to open its file.

The mountain · San Blas

Continuous mountain forest: poison frogs, harlequin toads, highland birds and elusive mammals.

The sea · Isla Popa & Bocas

Mangrove, reef and islands: the strawberry frog and its hundred morphs, nocturnal frogs and marine life.
